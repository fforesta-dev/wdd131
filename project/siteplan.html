Book of Mormon Names Explorer

Site Name

Book of Mormon Names Explorer

Why: clearly communicates a searchable, study-friendly explorer of Book of Mormon names, places, and terms. Optional domain: bom-names-explorer.net.

Site Purpose

A searchable, interactive glossary of Book of Mormon names, places, and key terms. Users can type a name to see a short description, scripture references, and pronunciation tips. Favorites are saved for quick review.

Scenarios

  • “Who is Alma the Younger and where is he mentioned?”
  • “How do I pronounce Zarahemla and what does it refer to?”
